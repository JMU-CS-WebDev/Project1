First blog: 9/27/2021

Hey everybody! Today starts another muraculus day in the life of a gym rat. Well atleast
thats what Id be saying if I didnt have to go to classes today haha. Today I worked on
"progressive overload" grrr. No dont be scared! Progressive overload is when you increase
your amount of reps or the weight you do for each excercise the more you go to the gym. So
today, I incorporated an additional 5 pounds to my squat and hit a new personal record(PR)
of 195lbs. Im so excited, because I had been stuck at a plateua of 190lbs for the last 2 or
so weeks. Id like to thank my new knee pads and belt for the helpfulness. The lifting belt
hepls keep your back straight in order to activate your core, and the knee pads help drive
your legs straight back up with full force!
Until next time - VegSharon
